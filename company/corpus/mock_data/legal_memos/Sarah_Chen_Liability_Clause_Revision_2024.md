### Background

Following the loss of NHS-SW-2022-DIG-047 (NHS SouthWest Digital Infrastructure Tender, rejected Oct 2022), I was tasked with producing a revised liability clause compliant with:

- NHS England Procurement Policy Note PPN 06/21
- NHS Digital Supplier Standards v4.2
- ICO Data Processor requirements under UK GDPR

The original Clause 7.3 (10% annual cap) caused the 2022 rejection. It was insufficient by a factor of 9x.

This memo establishes the mandatory replacement clause for all future NHS bids.

---

### The Problem Clause (DO NOT USE)

> **OLD Clause 7.3:**  
> "The total aggregate liability of the Supplier shall not exceed ten percent (10%) of the annual fees paid in the twelve (12) months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim."

**Why this fails NHS procurement:**
- Caps at ~£140K on a £4.2M contract
- No patient data carve-out
- No NHS Digital compliance language
- Below PPN 06/21 minimum threshold

---

### Revised Clause 7.3b — MANDATORY FROM Q1 2024

> **NEW Clause 7.3b:**  
> "The total aggregate liability of the Supplier under or in connection with this Agreement shall not exceed one hundred percent (100%) of the total fees payable under this Agreement over its full term. Notwithstanding the foregoing, the Supplier's liability shall be unlimited in respect of: (i) death or personal injury caused by negligence; (ii) fraud or fraudulent misrepresentation; (iii) any breach of obligations under the Data Protection Act 2018 or UK GDPR resulting in unauthorised disclosure of patient-identifiable data; and (iv) any liability that cannot be excluded by law. The Supplier shall maintain Professional Indemnity insurance of not less than £5,000,000 per claim throughout the Term and for a period of six (6) years thereafter."

---

### What Changed and Why

| Element | Old (DO NOT USE) | New (MANDATORY) |
|---------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Cap level | 10% annual fees | 100% total contract value |
| Patient data | Not mentioned | Unlimited liability carve-out |
| Insurance requirement | None specified | £5M PI insurance mandatory |
| NHS compliance | Generic | PPN 06/21 aligned |

---

### Application

This clause is **mandatory** for:
- All NHS Integrated Care Board bids
- All NHS Trust digital contracts
- Any public sector healthcare data contract

Sales must not submit NHS bids without Legal sign-off confirming Clause 7.3b is in place.

### Outcome Tracking

- **2024 Q1:** Clause 7.3b first applied — NHS Midlands Digital Pathways bid
- **2024 Q3:** NHS Midlands contract awarded. £3.8M. Procurement panel confirmed liability language compliant.
- **2024 Q4:** Clause applied to 3 additional NHS bids. All passed procurement stage.

The 2022 failure has not recurred since Clause 7.3b adoption.
