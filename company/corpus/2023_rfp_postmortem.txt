2023 RFP Postmortem: Meridian Bid

The Meridian bid was lost in 2023 after the customer review focused on liability caps in clause 7.4, indemnification language, and ambiguity in the risk-sharing framework. The deal desk noted that our preferred limitation of liability did not match the procurement team's expected commercial posture.

For future RFP responses, legal and sales should align early on indemnification, liability caps, clause numbering, and fallback language. A concise explanation of why clause 7.4 protects both sides should be included before final submission.
