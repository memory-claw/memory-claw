> "The total aggregate liability of the Supplier under or in connection with this Agreement, whether arising in contract, tort (including negligence), breach of statutory duty, or otherwise, shall not exceed ten percent (10%) of the annual fees paid in the twelve (12) months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim."

### Procurement Panel Notes (Internal — NHS SW Procurement)

*From: Emma Patel, NHS SW Procurement Lead*  
*To: Vendor File NHS-SW-2022-DIG-047*  
*Date: 2022-10-03*

Panel reviewed liability limitation at Clause 7.3. Vendor has applied a 10% annual cap which is materially below the NHS standard procurement threshold. NHS England guidance (PPN 06/21) requires minimum indemnity coverage equivalent to full contract value for data-handling suppliers. This submission does not meet the minimum standard.

Additionally, no NHS-specific indemnity carve-outs were included for patient data loss events. This is a mandatory requirement under NHS Digital supplier standards.

**Panel Recommendation: Reject at Stage 2 — Liability terms unacceptable. Vendor may resubmit with corrected terms in future cycle.**
