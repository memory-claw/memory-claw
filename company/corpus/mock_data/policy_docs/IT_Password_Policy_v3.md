# IT POLICY
## Password and Access Management Policy v3.2

**Author:** IT Team  
**Date:** 2024-03-01  
**Department:** IT  

All company systems now require passwords of minimum 14 characters with multi-factor authentication. Passwords must be changed every 90 days. Use of personal password managers is permitted. Shared passwords are prohibited. Access requests for new systems should be submitted via the IT helpdesk portal. Offboarding requests must be submitted within 24 hours of an employee's last day.
