### Agenda Item 1 — NHS Liability Clause Revision

Sarah presented draft Clause 7.3b. Background: the 2022 NHS SouthWest loss was caused entirely by an insufficient liability cap. Sarah has been working on a compliant replacement since November 2023.

**Tom Walker:** "Honestly I blocked this out. Lost us £4.2M."  
**Sarah Chen:** "The new clause sets the cap at 100% of total contract value. It's a significant change from our standard template but it's what NHS procurement requires."  
**Rachel Moore:** "What's the process for making sure this actually gets used?"  
**Sarah Chen:** "I'm proposing a mandatory sign-off gate. No NHS bid goes out without me or a delegated counsel confirming the clause is in."  
**James Liu:** "Same thing we did with pricing after FinCo. Seems right."

**Decision:** Clause 7.3b approved. Mandatory legal sign-off process for NHS bids adopted from Q1 2024.
